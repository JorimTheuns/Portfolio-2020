Dembrane

Dembrane

or New E-Democracy

The more we examine our universe, the more we come to understand that it is not governed by certainty, but by chance.


The Question is: if chance governs our societites, why fight back?


Dembrane is a new e-democracy platform that uses the counter-intuitive power of randomness to connect people to eachother, and to their governments.

Genesis

Is there a root cause to political polarisation?

The Story

It all started in 2018. After completing a project about how a far-right politician in Italy used social media to juxtapose Italian food with the ongoing migrant crisisin order to stoke "in/out-group salience", I realised that any reactionary response would only pour oil on the flames.

What is the root?

I asked myself: What if political polarisation is a symptom of an underlying, structural problem? Is there a root cause? If so, what is it? What would be an effective way to tackle it?
